Rough shooting is available on our farmland (not in the woods near Fisherman's Lodges) for rabbits and pigeons.

In order to shoot you must hold a valid gun license. No equipment is provided.

After your holiday rough shooting is generally available for an extra daily charge.

Clay Pigeon Shooting

Organised Clay Pigeon Shooting is available on a farm 3 miles away. Alternatively, for regular customers, we can provide an area for you to set up your own traps etc.
