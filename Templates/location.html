Our office is situated in Kenardington at our farm and we have properties in Kenardington, Woodchurch and Appledore.
The area sits at the edge of the Romney Marsh, which is famous for its beautiful, wild landscape. Nearby towns include Rye, Tenterden, Ashford, Canterbury and Tonbridge Wells. You can find more inspiration of things to do in the area here.

Getting here
Road

We are just 8 miles from Junction 10 of the M20.

Train

Ashford International is 10 miles away and can be reached in less than 40 minutes from London St Pancras.

Eurostar

If coming from the Continent the Eurostar stops at Ashford International, traveling from Brussels and France.

Ferry

The ferry port of Dover is less than an hour's drive away.

Plane

The major international airports of Gatwick and Heathrow can be reached in a couple of hours.
