A tranquil destination for your next holiday

Escape to the countryside with our country cottages and detached pine lodges set in bluebell woods overlooking 4 acres of water with a panoramic view of fields, woods and nearby picturesque village.

At Ashby Farms we have been providing quality self-catering accomodation for over 30 years. It's our priority to make your holiday as easy and enjoyable as possible.


We have a selection of properties available to suit every need; from families, to couples to large groups, and for everything from a short break to several months, perhaps while moving home.



Just 1.5 hours from London and major airports

Family and pet friendly

Properties available to sleep up to 6 people

Fishing in our beautiful lakes and rough shooting available
