Old Farm Flat

Old Farm Flat

This flat within a Grade II listed building gives you a chance to see authentic farm life as it is situated on a working farm among fields on the Romney Marsh.
The property has recently been refurbished and offers a great spot for a couple on solo traveller. It has one double bedroom, a fully-equipped kitchen, bathroom and a spacious living room. The living room has a wood fire, perfect for keeping cosy on colder evenings.
Old Farm offers a homely feel which makes it ideal for a longer stay. Perhaps while moving home. You can find out more about options for long lets here.

Location

It is a perfect base for exploring the beautiful Kent and East Sussex countryside, exploring the Cinque Port town of Rye or a jaunt to the coast. Find out more about things to do in the area here.

Shop: 3.2mi Pub: 3.2mi Coast: 8mi

Transport

The property offers plenty of space for parking.

Appledore has a train station which is a little away from the village centre with trains towards Ashford and Brighton. Ashford International offers services to London in 40 minutes.

Bus services are available from Appledore to Ashford and Tenterden.

There are a number of local taxi companies, details of these can be found here.

Heating

The propery has metered gas heating which must be paid for with 50p coins. It also has a wood fire
