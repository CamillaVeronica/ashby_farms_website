We have a selection of properties available for rent allowing you to get away from it all in the Kent countryside. Whether you want a rural retreat or a taste of village life we have options to suit for a weekend break through to longer stays.

Fisherman’s Lodges

Scandinavian-inspired lodges nestled alongside two fishing lakes surrounded by woodland close to the village of Woodchurch offer peace to unwind. Each property sleeps up to 6 people.

Find out more

Roughlands

Experience more privacy with this country bungalow with its own large garden, set well back from the quiet country road between Appledore and Woodchurch with Roughlands Wood and our fishing lakes in the woods on the North side and open fields on the others. Sleeps up to 5 people.

Find out more

Spring Cottages

These former farm workers’ cottages in the small village of Kenardington offer lots of space with 3 bedrooms and a taste of country life as the gardens back on to our pastures.

Find out more

65 The Street

Experience the charming village of Appledore, sometimes known as the gateway to Romney Marsh from this semi-detached property on the main street. Sleeps up to 5 people

Find out more

Old Farm Flat

Our smallest property is great for a couple or solo traveller and offers and authentic taste of farm life as it is situated on a working farm on Romney Marsh, your neighbour will even be the farm foreman.

Find out more

Group bookings

Booking a trip for a large group? We can arrange block booking of multiple properties to accommodate large groups of up to 48. Perfect for clubs, reunions, team building weekends, organisations or educational groups.

Long lets

As well as self-catering holidays we offer long lets of up to 5 months. Perfect for when looking for a new home, temporary relocation or during redecoration.
